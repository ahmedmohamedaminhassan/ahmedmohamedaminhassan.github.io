Stay at Home

It is becoming increasingly clear that the continuous transmission of the Coronavirus in society has taken hold around the world, so stay at home to eliminate the virus.

Social Distancing

Social distancing, also called " physical distancing" , means keeping a safe space between yourself and other people who are not from your household.

Wear Facemask

Masks are Not Perfect Barriers to Transmission, and Should be Combined with Other Preventative Measures Such as Social Distancing and Contact Tracing.

Wash Your Hands
